Writing Contest Guidelines:


     1. Entries should have a maximum of 500 words.

     2. Entries must be typed, doubled spaced using Times Roman font, 12 point.

     3. Only one entry per student

     4. Contestants must be in grades 6-12 for the 2017 – 2018 school year.

     5. Title page should include: name, grade, school, title of short story or poem and word count.

     6. No profanity will be allowed, any entries with profanity will be automatically disqualified.

     7. Entries must be submitted no later than June 30, 2018

     8. Entries must be submitted to the following email: NFLFwritingcontest2018@gmail.com

     9. Entries must be unpublished works.

     10. Scholarships will be awarded to the 3rd, 2nd, and 1st place winners in each category for both middle and high school contestants.

 

Poetry Topic:


Write a poem based on your belief about life after death.



Creative Topics:



Fiction:


Create a scene in a meadow involving three different races or species who have to overcome language or cultural barriers to decide if they are among friend or foe.


Or


Non-Fiction:


Write about something ugly like war, hatred or cruelty and look for the silver lining or something good that comes out of it.
