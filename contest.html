Writing Contest Guidelines:


  1. Entries should have a maximum of 500 words.

  2. Entries must be typed in 12-pt. Times New Roman font, and double-spaced.

  3. Only one entry per student

  4. Middle School contestants will be 6th - 8th grades, and High School contestants will be 9th - 12th grades.

  5. Title page should include: name, grade, school, title of entry, and word count.

  6. Any entry containing profanity will automatically be disqualified.

  7. Entries must be submitted no later than June 30, 2018.

  8. Entries must be submitted to: NFLFwritingcontest2018@gmail.com

  9. Entries must be unpublished works.

  10. High School Scholarship awards: 3rd place $25, 2nd place $50, and 3rd place $75. Middle School Scholarship awards: 3rd place $20, 2nd place $30, 3rd place $40.
