The Tallahassee Authors Network (TAN) is pleased to host the North Florida Literacy Festival in Tallahassee, Florida, on August 17-18, 2018. TAN, a group of published authors, aspiring writers, and avid readers, planned this festival to promote literacy generally, to benefit area literacy programs specifically, and to encourage the support of local authors and artists. School libraries in Leon, Jefferson, and Gadsden counties will be the greatest benefactors of this festival. TAN will award monies to select schools in each of these counties for the purchase of books.
